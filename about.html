About Me!

I'm Jayden Kimberlin, and I'm 15 years old. I started graphic designing around 2014, after discovering Evan Eckard, and 2 years after my mom graduated from Specs Howard. She becoming a graphic designer really sparked my interest for designing and creating my own artwork. Growing up, I always wanted to create my own art, focused around space. It was always an interest of mine, and it still is. Then around 2015, taking a break from design, I got into a lot of editing, filming, photography, and even longboard. Though not too big of a topic, my first longboard is the Omen Sugar. My first camera is a Canon Rebel T5. Photography was a hot hobby for me, which sparked my interest for design yet again, as I wanted to create everything I saw . I got into more complicated designs, such as, "Trippy Triangle", which I watched a tutorial to make it. From there on, I've experimented with all of my hobbies! All at once! Regardless of anything stopping me. Looking at you school, looking at you. That's a little about me!
